Excited about making my first post here toward the end of 2024. I think I will write about both my personal and professional lives here. Let's see.

It's been a year and a half of changes
- Jul 2023: Dad died
- Aug 2023: Started first computing course
- Oct 2023: Off-site in BKK with company
- Dec 2023: ROM
- Jan 2024: Went to China; started second computing course
- Mar 2024: Off-site in Phuket/BKK with company
- May 2024: Went to South Korea; Left my company and switched to an (academic) job
- Jun 2024: Decided to switch jobs again
- Sep/Oct 2024: Went to Japan; Got COVID; reservist
- Nov 2024: Started my new (data engineering) job

Amidst all the changes, I think I continued to learn and do things that are meaningful to me at this time, and I am glad for that. 

Most recently, I am happy about the first six weeks spent in my new job. I think I have already learnt a ton, and there's still a long, long way to go. Over the past weeks, I have:
- Created and/or migrated ETL pipelines using an ADF-like tool, and HiveQL/SparkSQL/PySpark
- Debugged pipelines involving PBI, SSIS
- Set up a CICD process with Azure DevOps Services and Databricks Asset Bundles (still work-in-progress)

Colleagues are collegial. Manager is flexible enough. All in all, pretty satisfied about it. Looking forward to the next few months!
